Who We Are

Japan Car Export (BRG AutoHub) is a trusted, Japan-based vehicle export company specializing in supplying high-quality, pre-owned cars to the Caribbean market. Based right in the heart of the Japanese automotive industry, we bridge the gap between world-class vehicle condition and Caribbean drivers.

Our mission is simple: To provide an incredibly transparent, secure, and hassle-free vehicle importing experience. Whether you are in Jamaica, Trinidad & Tobago, Barbados, Guyana, or the Bahamas, you will receive expert, dedicated service.
