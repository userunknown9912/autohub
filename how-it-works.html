Secure, Transparent Process

We have refined our export lifecycle into 4 straightforward steps. From selecting the perfect vehicle to watching it roll onto your local port, we handle everything with care and transparency.

1

Choose & Request

Browse our island favorites, monitor our daily Facebook posts, or request a precise, custom vehicle straight from the auction lines. We confirm condition and pricing before moving forward.

2

Confirm & Pay

Once you've chosen a vehicle, we issue an official Proforma Invoice. Complete your transaction securely via International TT Bank Transfer.

3

Shipping

We organize space aboard RoRo or Container vessels on the earliest available schedule. Original documents (BL, Export Cert) are then couriered via DHL.

4

Clear & Receive

Your vehicle arrives at the destination port. Present the documents to your local customs broker to clear and drive your vehicle home.
