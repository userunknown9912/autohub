Shipping & Logistics

Safe, scheduled, and fully-insured transport straight to your port.

RoRo Shipping

Roll-On / Roll-Off is the most popular, cost-effective, and efficient way to transport vehicles. Cars are driven securely onto specialized vessels, strapped tightly to the specialized deck floors.

Ideal for:

  • Single car imports
  • Standard sedans and SUVs
  • Faster clearance off the boat

Container Shipping

Vehicles are loaded and firmly lashed block-stowed directly inside a 20ft or 40ft maritime container before it's loaded onto a cargo ship. Offers elevated protection against external elements.

Ideal for:

  • Multiple vehicles (vanging loading)
  • High-value luxury/performance cars
  • Shipping extra parts within vehicle

Essential Documentation

Before the ship reaches port, you will receive original physical copies of vital documents via DHL Express courier to successfully clear your car via your country's customs border agency.

Bill of Lading (B/L)

Issued by the carrier. Acts as a receipt of freight services and the rightful contract of carriage granting you ownership.

Export Certificate

An official deregistration/cancellation certificate translating Japanese history, chassis, model, and year directly from the Japanese Department of Transport.

Commercial Invoice

Specifies the exact value and customs classifications of the imported vehicle, necessary for precise tax calculation in the Caribbean.
